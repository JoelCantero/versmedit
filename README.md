# versmedit

## How it works

Here’s a quick tutorial on how to use Versmedit.

First, add one or more verses in My Account page.

The regularity of repetition is based on the Leitner System.

If you remember a verse in each repetition, it takes about 120 days, around four months, to reach the "Mastered" state.

This precise timing is not a coincidence. It is what makes the Leitner system work so well. Each interval is designed to strengthen memory just before you forget, neither too early nor too late.

The intervals are as follows:

- Level 1 -> 2: the same day (immediately)
- Level 2 -> 3: after 2 days
- Level 3 -> 4: after 3 days
- Level 4 -> 5: after 7 days
- Level 5 -> 6: after 15 days
- Level 6 -> 7: after 31 days
- Level 7 -> Mastered: after 61 days

Therefore, when users ask:
"Why can't I change the schedule?"
the answer is simple:

Because changing it would break the magic. The power of the Leitner method lies in its rhythm — the perfect balance between forgetting and remembering.

Each verse has its own journey. Trust the schedule and it will lead you to mastery. 🚀

## FAQ

### Learning process

#### How does daily learning work?

Every day, the system selects cards from your box based on the Leitner schedule. The goal is to review cards at optimal intervals for better retention.

#### What happens when I know a verse?

- If you **know the verse** and do not any mistake, the verse moves to the next level.

- If you **don’t know the verse** nor do mistakes, the verse returns to Level 1 for more frequent review.

#### What if I forget a verse while studying?

No worries! If you forget a verse, just return it to the first level and start again from the beginning. Repetition helps you hide God’s Word in your heart. The more you practice, the stronger and more natural the verse becomes in your memory.

#### How often should I study my verses?

When memorizing Scripture with the Leitner system, it’s important to follow your review schedule consistently. Some verses will appear daily, while others will come up weekly or less often as you progress. Trust the process—each verse is reviewed at the right time to help you remember it long-term.

#### Why don't I have any verses to study today?

Verses appear for review based on your Leitner schedule. The higher a verse’s level, the less often it needs to be reviewed. If no verses show up today, it simply means you’re on track—nothing is due yet. Keep going tomorrow!

#### How often are verses available for study?

Each verse follows its own individual schedule based on its level:

- **Level 1 → 2**: same day (immediately)  
- **Level 2 → 3**: after 2 days  
- **Level 3 → 4**: after 3 days  
- **Level 4 → 5**: after 7 days  
- **Level 5 → 6**: after 15 days  
- **Level 6 → 7**: after 31 days  
- **Level 7 → Mastered**: after 61 days  

This personalized schedule ensures that each verse is reviewed at the optimal time, helping you retain it more effectively and store it in your heart long-term.

#### What happens if I miss one or two days?

Each verse follows its own individual schedule, so there’s no strict “24-hour window” you need to worry about.

If you miss a day or two of practice, your verses will not disappear or reset. They will simply remain due, and you can continue your memorization whenever you return. This way, your progress stays intact—even if you skip a couple of days.

#### How long does it usually take to memorize a verse from Level 1 to Mastered?

If you consistently recall a verse correctly, it will progress through all levels in about 120 days — roughly four months — before reaching the Mastered stage.

#### Can I study mastered verses again?

Yes, you can review your mastered verses.

To do this, select the mastered verses and tap the **Study Again** button. They will return to Level 1 and begin the memorization process from the beginning.
